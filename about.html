About R&R by MHB LLC

Who We Are

R&R by MHB LLC is a family-owned renovation and contracting business based in Brunswick, Ohio. We proudly serve homeowners throughout Cuyahoga, Medina, and Summit Counties.

Our Experience

Owner Jag Sookhdeo brings over 30 years of experience remodeling homes — from kitchen and bathroom renovations to custom decks, siding, flooring, and more.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide quality work at affordable prices. Every project is tailored to fit your budget while maintaining the highest standards of craftsmanship and reliability.

What Makes Us Different

We believe in honesty, communication, and attention to detail. Every job comes backed by our limited service warranty and a commitment to making your home improvement experience stress-free and successful.
